About BookingHub

Comprehensive booking and subscription management system

System Overview

What is BookingHub?

BookingHub is a comprehensive booking and subscription management system designed for multi-service businesses including gyms, salons, co-working spaces, and wellness centers. It provides a unified platform to manage appointments, memberships, payments, and customer relationships.

Key Features

  • 📅 Advanced calendar management with staff and resource scheduling
  • 👥 Comprehensive member profiles with plan and usage tracking
  • 💳 Flexible membership plans and class pack management
  • 💰 Integrated billing and payment processing
  • 📊 Detailed analytics and business insights
  • 🖨️ Print-ready invoices, rosters, and schedules
