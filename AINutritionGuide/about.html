‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the AI food recognition?

Our AI recognition system is trained on millions of food images and achieves over 90% accuracy for common foods. However, accuracy may vary for unusual dishes or poor lighting conditions. You can always manually adjust the recognition results.

Can I use the app without an internet connection?

Yes! The core functionality works offline. Food recognition requires an internet connection, but you can log meals manually and view your diary without connectivity.

How do I sync with my fitness tracker?

Go to the Integrations page and select your fitness app. Follow the authorization process to connect your accounts. You can choose which data to sync and can disconnect at any time.

Is my data secure and private?

Absolutely. In this prototype version, all data is stored locally on your device. Photos are never uploaded to external servers. When cloud sync is implemented, all data will be encrypted and you'll have full control over what gets shared.

Can I export my nutrition data?

Yes! You can export your data in CSV or JSON format from the Settings page. This includes all logged meals, nutrition data, and progress information for backup or analysis purposes.
