Quarterly Insight Report - Q3 2024
Executive Summary

Q3 2024 showed strong user engagement with 1,247 feedback items collected across all channels. Our Net Promoter Score improved to +41 (+2 vs Q2), while Customer Satisfaction remained stable at 4.3/5. The top themes this quarter focused on checkout reliability, export performance, and mobile user experience.

Key initiatives launched include dark mode for mobile apps (addressing 44 feedback items) and improved error handling in checkout flows. The team successfully maintained 78% tag coverage despite 15% growth in feedback volume.
