💡 Local Tips

Best Times to Visit

  • • Early morning (7-9 AM) for outdoor activities
  • • Late afternoon (4-6 PM) for shopping and cafes
  • • Evening (7-9 PM) for dining and nightlife
  • • Avoid midday (12-2 PM) due to heat

Transport Tips

  • • Get EZ-Link card for MRT/bus
  • • Use Grab app for taxis
  • • Most attractions within 30min by MRT
  • • Walking is often faster than driving

Food Tips

  • • Hawker centers for authentic local food
  • • Bring tissues for table reservation
  • • Try chicken rice, laksa, and satay
  • • Most places accept contactless payment

Cultural Tips

  • • Remove shoes in temples
  • • Dress modestly for religious sites
  • • No chewing gum in public
  • • Be respectful of local customs
