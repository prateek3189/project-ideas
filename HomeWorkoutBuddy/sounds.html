Sound Tips

  • Brown noise reduces harsh highs; many find it gentler than white noise
  • Start at a low volume and gradually increase if needed
  • Mix different sounds for a personalized experience
  • Use a timer to automatically stop sounds after you fall asleep
  • Experiment with different combinations to find what works best
  • Consider using headphones for better sound isolation

Volume Control

Adjust the volume to a level that's just audible enough to mask background noise without being distracting.
