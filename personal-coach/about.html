Your compassionate companion for sustainable growth

Our Mission

AI Personal Coach helps busy professionals build sustainable habits across fitness, productivity, and leadership. We believe in compassionate coaching that adapts to your energy, schedule, and goalsâ€”never shaming, always supporting.

This is a prototype that demonstrates how AI-powered personal coaching could work. All "AI" content is currently static, but the framework is designed to support real AI integration in the future.

Methodology

Evidence-Based Approach

  • Habit Stacking: Building new habits on existing routines
  • Energy-Based Planning: Adapting intensity to your daily energy levels
  • Micro-Actions: Breaking goals into achievable daily steps
  • Streak Protection: Grace periods and recovery days to prevent burnout
  • Context Awareness: Considering your schedule, mood, and environment

Compassionate Coaching

  • No Shame: Missed days are data, not failures
  • Flexible Plans: Adjust without guilt when life happens
  • Progress Focus: Celebrate small wins and trends
  • Recovery Support: Built-in rest and adaptation periods

Privacy & Data

Local-First Design

This prototype stores all data locally on your device. No data is sent to external servers, ensuring complete privacy.
