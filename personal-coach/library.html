Coach Library

Playbooks are proven tactics. Pick one and try it for a week.

Productivity Playbooks

Timeboxing

Productivity

Allocate specific time blocks for different activities. Prevents over-optimization and creates natural boundaries.

How to implement
  • Block 25-90 min chunks for deep work
  • Use 15-min buffers between blocks
  • Color-code by activity type
  • Review and adjust weekly

Pomodoro 50/10

Productivity

Extended Pomodoro technique: 50 minutes of focused work, 10 minutes of rest. Better for complex tasks.

How to implement
  • Set timer for 50 minutes
  • Work on single task only
  • Take 10-min break (walk, stretch)
  • Repeat 3-4 cycles max

Kill-Switch for Distractions

Productivity

Create physical and digital barriers to common distractions during focus time.

How to implement
  • Phone in another room
  • Website blockers for social media
  • Do Not Disturb mode
  • Physical "focus zone" setup

Fitness Playbooks

Zone 2 Basics

Fitness

Low-intensity cardio that builds aerobic base. You should be able to hold a conversation while exercising.

How to implement
  • 30-60 minutes, 2-3x per week
  • Walking, cycling, or swimming
  • Heart rate 60-70% of max
  • Conversation test: can you talk?

Protein Target Guide

Fitness

Simple protein targets to support muscle maintenance and recovery. No complex tracking required.

How to implement
  • 25-30g per meal (3 meals)
  • Palm-sized portion of meat/fish
  • Greek yogurt or protein shake
  • Track for 1 week to build habit

Mobility-8

Fitness

8-minute daily mobility routine to improve flexibility and reduce injury risk.

How to implement
  • Do first thing in morning
  • 8 exercises, 1 minute each
  • Focus on major joints
  • No equipment needed

Leadership Playbooks

SBI Feedback

Leadership

Situation-Behavior-Impact framework for giving clear, actionable feedback.

How to implement
  • Situation: When and where
  • Behavior: What they did
  • Impact: How it affected others
  • Use for both praise and improvement

Weekly 1:1 Template

Leadership

Structured agenda for meaningful 1:1 conversations that build trust and drive results.

How to implement
  • Wins: What went well?
  • Blocks: What's in the way?
  • Asks: What do you need?
  • 30 minutes, weekly cadence

Decision Log

Leadership

Track important decisions to improve future decision-making and team transparency.

How to implement
  • Date, decision, context
  • Who was involved
  • Expected outcome
  • Review monthly
