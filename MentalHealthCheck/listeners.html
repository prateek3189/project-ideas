Sample Dialogue Examples

Seeker:

"I'm feeling really overwhelmed with work and I don't know what to do."

Good Listener Response:

"I can hear that you're feeling overwhelmed with work. That sounds really challenging. Would you like to tell me more about what's been going on?"

Why this works:

  • ‚Ä¢ Validates the emotion (overwhelmed)
  • ‚Ä¢ Shows empathy and understanding
  • ‚Ä¢ Asks an open-ended question

  • ‚Ä¢ Clearly states boundaries
  • ‚Ä¢ Redirects to appropriate resources
  • ‚Ä¢ Maintains supportive tone
