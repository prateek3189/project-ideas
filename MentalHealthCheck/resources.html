üßò Grounding Exercises

These exercises can help you feel more centered and present when you're feeling overwhelmed, anxious, or disconnected.

4-7-8 Breathing

Inhale for 4 counts, hold for 7, exhale for 8. Repeat 4 times.

4 - 7 - 8

This breathing pattern activates your parasympathetic nervous system, helping you feel calmer.

5-4-3-2-1 Senses

Name 5 things you see, 4 you hear, 3 you touch, 2 you smell, 1 you taste.

5 - 4 - 3 - 2 - 1

This exercise helps ground you in the present moment using your five senses.

Box Breathing

Inhale for 4, hold for 4, exhale for 4, hold for 4. Repeat.

Progressive Muscle Relaxation

Tense and release each muscle group from head to toe.

üíô Self-Care & Wellness

Sleep Hygiene

Create a bedtime routine, avoid screens 1 hour before bed, keep your room cool and dark.

Stress Management

Identify triggers, practice relaxation techniques, maintain healthy boundaries.

Mindfulness & Meditation

Practice present-moment awareness through guided meditations and mindfulness exercises.

Physical Activity

Regular exercise can improve mood, reduce anxiety, and boost overall wellbeing.

Healthy Eating

Nourish your body with balanced meals and stay hydrated throughout the day.

Social Connection

Maintain relationships with supportive friends and family members.
