About Skill Builder

Learn more about our micro-course approach

Our Mission

Skill Builder transforms learning into bite-sized, achievable experiences. We believe that the best learning happens in small, focused increments that fit into your busy life.

Our micro-courses are designed to be completed in 5-10 minutes, making them perfect for coffee breaks, commutes, or any spare moment you have to invest in yourself.

Accessibility & Inclusive Design

We're committed to making learning accessible to everyone. Our platform is designed with accessibility in mind, featuring:

  • Semantic HTML structure for screen readers
  • High contrast themes and customizable font sizes
  • Keyboard navigation support
  • Alternative text for all images and diagrams
  • Focus indicators for interactive elements
  • Print-friendly styles for offline learning

We continuously work to improve accessibility and welcome feedback from our community.
