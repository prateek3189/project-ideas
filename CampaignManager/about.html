Campaign Manager v0.1

Prototype

Campaign Manager is a comprehensive marketing campaign management platform designed to help businesses create, schedule, and track email and SMS marketing campaigns. This is a static HTML + CSS prototype that demonstrates the complete user experience without requiring JavaScript or backend services.

Key Features

  • • Campaign Creation: 5-step wizard for building campaigns
  • • Audience Management: Segment contacts and manage suppression lists
  • • Template Library: Pre-built email and SMS templates
  • • Journey Builder: Visual automation workflows
  • • Performance Reports: Detailed analytics and insights
  • • Compliance Tools: Built-in consent management and opt-out handling

Compliance & Legal

Important Notice

This is a prototype demonstration. All data shown is fictional and for display purposes only.

Privacy & Data Protection

GDPR Compliance

Built-in consent management, right to be forgotten, and data portability features.

CAN-SPAM Act

Automatic unsubscribe links, clear sender identification, and physical address requirements.

TCPA Compliance

Express written consent tracking and opt-out mechanisms for SMS campaigns.

CASL Compliance

Canadian Anti-Spam Legislation compliance with proper consent and identification.

Deliverability Best Practices

  • • Domain Authentication: SPF, DKIM, and DMARC record configuration
  • • List Hygiene: Automatic bounce handling and suppression list management
  • • Content Guidelines: Spam filter optimization and text-to-image ratio monitoring
  • • Send Time Optimization: Respect for recipient time zones and quiet hours
  • • Engagement Tracking: Monitor open rates, click rates, and unsubscribe rates
