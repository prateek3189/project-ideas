Learning Tips

Read Together First

Read the story to your child first, then let them repeat key words and phrases.

Use Actions

Add hand movements and facial expressions to make stories and rhymes more engaging.

Practice Daily

Short, regular sessions are better than long, occasional ones. Aim for 10-15 minutes daily.

Celebrate Progress

Praise effort and celebrate small achievements. Learning should be fun and rewarding.
