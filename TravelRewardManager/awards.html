Search Tips

Best Time to Search

Award availability is typically released 11-12 months in advance. Check multiple times as airlines release additional seats closer to departure.

Flexible Dates

Use flexible date searches to find better availability. Mid-week flights often have more award seats than weekends.

Alternative Airports

Consider nearby airports for better availability. For Tokyo, check both NRT and HND. For New York, try JFK, LGA, and EWR.

Partner Airlines

Search partner airline websites for better availability. Star Alliance partners often have different award inventory.
