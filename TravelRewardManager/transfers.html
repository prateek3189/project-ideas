Transfer Tips

Transfer Timing

Transfer points only when you're ready to book. Most transfers are irreversible, so avoid transferring speculatively.

Bonus Opportunities

Watch for transfer bonuses that can increase your point value by 15-30%. These are often limited-time offers.

Transfer Times

Most transfers are instant, but some can take 1-2 days. Plan accordingly if you need points for a time-sensitive booking.

Minimum Transfers

Most programs require minimum transfer amounts (usually 1,000-5,000 points). Check requirements before initiating transfers.
