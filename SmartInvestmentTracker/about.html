About This Application

Smart Investment Tracker is a static HTML/CSS prototype designed to demonstrate portfolio management concepts and investment analysis techniques. This educational tool showcases:

  • Portfolio Visualization: Asset allocation charts, performance metrics, and diversification analysis
  • Risk Assessment: Concentration analysis, correlation studies, and risk scoring
  • Rebalancing Suggestions: AI-style recommendations for portfolio optimization
  • Market Analysis: Sector performance, factor analysis, and market sentiment indicators
  • Goal Tracking: Financial goal setting and progress monitoring
  • Transaction Management: Investment history and cost analysis

This prototype uses static data and placeholder calculations to illustrate investment management concepts. All data, prices, and calculations are for demonstration purposes only.

Methodology & Calculations

Portfolio Analysis

Asset Allocation

Calculated as percentage of total portfolio value. Target vs actual allocation drift is measured as the absolute difference between current and target percentages.

Diversification Score

Composite score (0-10) based on asset class diversity, geographic distribution, sector allocation, and individual holding concentration.

Risk Assessment

Evaluates concentration risk, correlation analysis, and volatility metrics to assess portfolio risk levels.

Rebalancing Logic

Suggestion Generation

AI-style suggestions are based on predefined rules for drift thresholds, concentration limits, and risk management principles.

Impact Analysis

Estimates the impact of rebalancing actions on risk, fees, and expected returns using simplified models.

Tax Considerations

Identifies potential tax implications of rebalancing actions, including capital gains and tax-loss harvesting opportunities.

Data Sources & Limitations

Static Data

This prototype uses static, placeholder data for demonstration purposes. No real-time market data or live prices are used.

Sample Holdings

The portfolio includes sample holdings such as VTI, VXUS, BND, and others for educational demonstration. These are not recommendations.

Market Data

Market trends, sector performance, and economic indicators shown are simplified placeholders and should not be used for actual investment decisions.

Calculations

All calculations, including returns, correlations, and risk metrics, are based on simplified models and placeholder data.
