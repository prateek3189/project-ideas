How It Works

1. Create Your Profile

Build your profile with skills you can teach and skills you want to learn. Add your availability, location, and any relevant experience or certifications.

2. Post Offers & Requests

Create offers for skills you can teach or requests for skills you want to learn. Be specific about what you're offering and what you're looking for.

3. Find Matches

Our platform shows you compatible matches based on your skills, location, and availability. Review profiles and compatibility scores.

4. Make Proposals

Send proposals to potential trading partners. Outline the exchange details, schedule, and expectations for both parties.

5. Trade Skills

Meet up (in person or online) and exchange skills. Track your sessions and earn time credits for future trades.

Safety & Trust

Profile Verification

  • Email verification required
  • Phone number verification for in-person meetings
  • Identity verification for high-value trades
  • Skill certification uploads encouraged

Safety Guidelines

  • Always meet in public places for first-time trades
  • Bring a friend or family member if you feel uncomfortable
  • Trust your instincts - if something feels wrong, don't proceed
  • Report any suspicious behavior immediately
  • Never share personal financial information

Trust Indicators

  • Reference system from previous trades
  • Time credit history and balance
  • Community badges for active members
  • Response time and communication quality

Time Credits System

How Time Credits Work

Time credits are our currency for skill exchanges. When you teach someone, you earn credits. When you learn from someone, you spend credits.

Earning Credits

  • 1 hour of teaching = 1 time credit
  • Bonus credits for first-time trades
  • Referral bonuses for bringing new members
  • Community contribution rewards

Spending Credits

  • 1 hour of learning = 1 time credit
  • Group sessions may cost fewer credits per person
  • Premium skills may cost additional credits

Credit Policies

  • Credits expire after 12 months of inactivity
  • No refunds for completed trades
  • Disputes handled by community moderators
  • Fraudulent activity results in account suspension

Community Guidelines

Prohibited Activities
