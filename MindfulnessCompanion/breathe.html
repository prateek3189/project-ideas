Breathing Tips

Nose Breathing

Breathe through your nose to filter air, warm it, and activate your parasympathetic nervous system for relaxation.

Relax Your Shoulders

Keep your shoulders relaxed and down. Tension in the upper body can restrict natural breathing patterns.

Belly Breathing

Allow your belly to expand on inhale and contract on exhale. This engages your diaphragm for deeper, more efficient breathing.

Consistent Rhythm

Maintain a steady, consistent rhythm. The regularity helps calm your nervous system and focus your mind.

Quick Breathing Exercises

Emergency Reset (1 minute)

Take 4 deep breaths: inhale for 4 counts, hold for 4, exhale for 4, hold for 4. Repeat 4 times.

Energy Boost (2 minutes)

Quick, energizing breaths: inhale for 2 counts, exhale for 2 counts. Increase pace gradually.

Sleep Prep (3 minutes)

Slow, deep breathing: inhale for 6 counts, exhale for 8 counts. Focus on the exhale being longer.
