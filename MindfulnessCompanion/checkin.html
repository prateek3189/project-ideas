Tips for Meaningful Check-ins

Be Honest

There's no right or wrong way to feel. Honest self-assessment helps you understand your patterns and needs.

Notice Patterns

Over time, you'll see connections between your stress levels, mood, and body sensations.

Be Kind

Approach your check-ins with curiosity and compassion, not judgment. You're learning about yourself.

Use as a Tool

Your check-ins can guide you to choose the right practices for your current state.
