Tips for Your Practice

Find Your Space

Choose a quiet, comfortable place where you won't be interrupted. Even a few minutes of undisturbed practice can be transformative.

Start Small

Begin with shorter sessions and gradually increase duration. Consistency matters more than length.

Be Kind to Yourself

Thoughts will wander - that's normal. Gently return to your focus without judgment. Every return is a moment of mindfulness.

Practice Regularly

Even 5 minutes daily is more beneficial than an hour once a week. Build a habit that fits your life.
