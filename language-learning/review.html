How Spaced Repetition Works

New Cards

Cards you're seeing for the first time. Review them daily until you're comfortable.

Daily A1

Learning Cards

Cards you're actively learning. Review them every few days to strengthen memory.

Every 2-3 days A2

Review Cards

Cards you know well. Review them at increasing intervals to maintain long-term retention.

Weekly/Monthly B1

Study Tips for Better Retention

🎯 Focus on Difficult Cards

Spend more time on cards you find challenging. The algorithm will show them more frequently.

⏰ Study in Short Sessions

15-20 minute sessions are more effective than long study marathons. Review daily for best results.

🗣️ Practice Speaking

Don't just read the phrases - say them out loud to improve pronunciation and retention.

📚 Use Context

Try to use phrases in conversations or create sentences with them to better understand usage.
