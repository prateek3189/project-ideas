About Smart Task Manager

Intelligent task prioritization powered by your mood and energy

Our Mission

Smart Task Manager revolutionizes productivity by understanding that the best task to work on isn't just the most urgent one‚Äîit's the task that matches your current mood, energy level, and available time.

We believe that productivity isn't about forcing yourself to work harder, but about working smarter by aligning your tasks with your natural rhythms and mental state.

What is Smart Priority?

Smart Priority is our AI-powered task ranking system that considers multiple factors beyond traditional deadline-based prioritization:

  • Deadline Urgency - How soon the task is due
  • Task Effort Level - Whether it's a quick win or deep work
  • Your Current Mood - Calm, focused, creative, or stressed
  • Your Energy Level - Low, medium, or high mental/physical energy
  • Historical Patterns - When you're most productive with similar tasks

The result is a priority score (0-100) that helps you choose the right task for the right moment, maximizing both productivity and well-being.

Accessibility Commitment

Smart Task Manager is designed to be accessible to all users, regardless of their abilities or the technology they use:

  • WCAG 2.1 AA Compliance - Meeting web accessibility standards
  • Keyboard Navigation - Full functionality without a mouse
  • Screen Reader Support - Semantic HTML and ARIA labels
  • High Contrast Mode - For users with visual impairments
  • Reduced Motion - Respects user preferences for motion
  • Print Accessibility - Clean, readable printed outputs

We continuously test with assistive technologies and gather feedback from users with disabilities to improve accessibility.
