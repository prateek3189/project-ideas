Runners Club

About This Group

Join our vibrant running community! We organize regular morning runs, fitness challenges, and social events. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced runner, you'll find your pace with us.

Regular Schedule:

  • Tuesday: 6:00 AM - Community Center
  • Thursday: 6:00 AM - Central Park
  • Saturday: 7:00 AM - Long run (various locations)

What to expect:

  • All fitness levels welcome
  • Group warm-up and cool-down
  • Post-run refreshments
  • Monthly social events

Group Rules

  • Be respectful to all members
  • Follow safety guidelines
  • Inform group if you can't make it
  • Help newcomers feel welcome
