Indie Night @ Blue Door

About This Event

Join us for an unforgettable evening of indie music at the iconic Blue Door Cafe. This intimate venue will host some of Mumbai's most talented indie artists, creating an atmosphere that's both cozy and electrifying.

Whether you're a long-time indie music fan or just discovering the scene, this event promises to deliver an authentic experience with great music, delicious food, and a community of music lovers.

What to Expect:

  • Live performances from 3 talented indie artists
  • Acoustic and electric sets
  • Delicious food and drinks available
  • Intimate venue with great acoustics
  • Meet and greet with artists after the show
