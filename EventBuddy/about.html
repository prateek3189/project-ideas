About Event Buddy

Connecting communities through amazing events

Our Mission

Event Buddy is dedicated to making it easy for people to discover, join, and organize amazing local events. We believe that great experiences happen when communities come together, and we're here to make that connection seamless.

Organize

Create and manage your own events with powerful tools for organizers of all sizes.

Community Guidelines

Creating a Safe Environment

We're committed to fostering a welcoming and inclusive community. All events and interactions should follow our community guidelines to ensure everyone has a positive experience.

Do's

  • • Be respectful and inclusive
  • • Provide accurate event information
  • • Follow local laws and regulations
  • • Create accessible events when possible
  • • Respond to attendee questions promptly

Don'ts

  • • Discriminate or harass others
  • • Create misleading or false events
  • • Violate intellectual property rights
  • • Engage in illegal activities
  • • Spam or abuse the platform

Anti-Scalping Policy

We strictly prohibit ticket scalping and resale at inflated prices. All ticket transfers must be done through our official platform at face value. Violators will be banned from the platform.

Refund Policy

Standard Refund Policy

Full refunds are available up to 24 hours before the event start time. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to the original payment method.

Event Cancellation

If an event is cancelled by the organizer, all attendees will receive a full refund automatically. No action required on your part.

Weather & Force Majeure

For outdoor events affected by severe weather or other force majeure events, organizers may offer rescheduled dates or partial refunds at their discretion.
