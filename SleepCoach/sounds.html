Sound Tips

Brown noise reduces harsh highs

Many find it gentler than white noise. It has more low-frequency content that can be more soothing.

Mix sounds for variety

Combine nature sounds with music or noise for a more complex, engaging soundscape.

Start at low volume

Begin with a gentle volume and adjust as needed. Too loud can be stimulating rather than calming.

Use a timer

Set sounds to fade out after you're likely to be asleep to avoid disrupting your sleep cycle.
