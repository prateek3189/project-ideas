About Sleep Coach

Learn about our approach to better sleep and how the app works.

What is Sleep Coach?

Sleep Coach is a static HTML + CSS prototype designed to help you build better sleep habits through pattern analysis, personalized wind-down routines, and soothing soundscapes.

This is a JavaScript-free v1 prototype that focuses on visual design and user experience. All analytics, timers, sensors, and audio logic are placeholders that can be enhanced with real functionality later.

Not Medical Advice
Sleep Coach is for educational purposes only and should not replace professional medical advice.

How Sleep Coach Works

1. Pattern Analysis

Track your sleep patterns to understand what works best for you. The app analyzes bedtime consistency, sleep duration, and quality factors.

2. Personalized Routines

Build custom wind-down routines with step-by-step guidance. Choose from presets or create your own based on your lifestyle.

3. Soothing Sounds

Access calming music, white noise, and nature sounds to help you fall asleep faster and stay asleep longer.

4. Smart Insights

Get personalized recommendations based on your sleep data, including chronotype analysis and sleep quality factors.

Sleep Hygiene Guide

Evidence-based tips for better sleep

Environment

  • • Keep bedroom cool (65-68°F/18-20°C)
  • • Use blackout curtains or eye mask
  • • Minimize noise or use white noise
  • • Keep bedroom for sleep and intimacy only

Timing

  • • Go to bed and wake up at the same time daily
  • • Avoid naps after 3 PM
  • • Don't lie in bed awake for more than 20 minutes
  • • Get sunlight exposure in the morning

Lifestyle

  • • Exercise regularly, but not close to bedtime
  • • Avoid caffeine 6-8 hours before bed
  • • Limit alcohol consumption
  • • Don't eat large meals before bed

Technology

  • • Avoid screens 1 hour before bed
  • • Use blue light filters if needed
  • • Keep devices out of the bedroom
  • • Use "Do Not Disturb" mode

  • • High contrast theme
