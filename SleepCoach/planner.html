Routine Tips

Consistency is key

Follow the same routine every night, even on weekends.

Start early

Begin your wind-down 45-60 minutes before your target bedtime.

Keep it simple

Too many steps can feel overwhelming. Start with 3-4 activities.

No screens

Avoid phones, tablets, and TVs during your wind-down.
