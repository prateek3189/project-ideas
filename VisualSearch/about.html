About Visual Search

Find similar items from photos using advanced visual recognition technology

How It Works

1. Upload or Capture

Take a photo or upload an image of the item you want to find similar products for.

2. AI Analysis

Our visual recognition system analyzes the image to identify objects, colors, shapes, and patterns.

3. Find Matches

Get a curated list of similar items with confidence scores and detailed explanations.
