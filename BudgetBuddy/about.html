About Budget Buddy

Your personal finance companion for effortless expense tracking

What is Budget Buddy?

Budget Buddy is a personal finance application designed to make expense tracking effortless and automatic. By parsing SMS messages from your bank and UPI transactions, it automatically categorizes your spending and provides insights into your financial habits.

The app focuses on privacy-first design - all data processing happens locally on your device. No personal financial information is sent to external servers, giving you complete control over your data.

How It Works

Budget Buddy uses advanced text parsing algorithms to extract transaction information from your SMS messages and UPI data.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority. Budget Buddy is designed with privacy-first principles to ensure your financial data remains secure and private.

Data Collection

  • No Personal Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information
  • Local Processing Only: All SMS parsing and data analysis happens on your device
  • No Account Access: We never access your bank accounts or financial institutions
  • No Data Sharing: Your data is never shared with third parties

Data Storage

  • Local Storage: All data is stored locally on your device
  • Encryption: Sensitive data is encrypted using device-level security
  • Data Masking: Account numbers and sensitive information are masked in displays
  • User Control: You can export, delete, or modify your data at any time

Permissions

  • SMS Access: Required to read bank SMS messages for transaction parsing
  • Storage Access: Required to store your financial data locally
  • No Network Access: The app does not require internet connectivity
✅ Privacy Guarantee: Your financial data never leaves your device. We cannot access, view, or share your personal information.

Important Disclaimers

⚠️ Financial Advice Disclaimer

Budget Buddy is a personal finance tracking tool and does not provide financial advice. All insights, suggestions, and analysis are for informational purposes only. Please consult with a qualified financial advisor for personalized financial advice.

⚠️ Data Accuracy Disclaimer

While Budget Buddy strives for accuracy in parsing SMS messages and categorizing transactions, the automated nature of the process means errors may occur. Always verify important transactions against your official bank statements.

⚠️ Bank Statement Verification

Budget Buddy is designed to complement, not replace, your official bank statements. Always refer to your bank's official records for accurate account balances and transaction details.

⚠️ SMS Parsing Limitations

SMS parsing accuracy depends on the format and content of your bank's messages. Different banks may use varying formats, and some transactions may not be parsed correctly. Manual review and correction may be necessary.

⚠️ Software Limitations

Budget Buddy is provided "as is" without warranties of any kind. We do not guarantee uninterrupted service or error-free operation. Users are responsible for backing up their data and verifying transaction accuracy.

🚨 Important: Always verify critical financial information with your bank. Budget Buddy is a tracking tool, not a replacement for official banking services.
